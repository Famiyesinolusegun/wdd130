History

Rafting has provided high-quality, affordable rafting experiences for outdoor enthusiasts across the country. Starting with humble roots as a small, locally owned operation, we've expanded our reach to include multiple locations throughout the western United States.

Throughout our growth, however, we've remained committed to upholding the values that define us: safety above all else; exceptional customer service; and a steadfast dedication to preserving the beauty of the great outdoors.With experienced guides leading the charge and cutting-edge equipment ensuring maximum comfort and security.
